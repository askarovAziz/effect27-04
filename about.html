Our Story

Established in 2018 • Professional Wellness Spa in Dubai

Effect Spa is a professional wellness and day spa based in Dubai, United Arab Emirates, operating under the legal entity EFFECT GENTS SALON L.L.C.

Established in 2018, Effect Spa was created with a clear goal: to provide a professional, well-managed, and comfortable spa environment where guests can relax with confidence.

Our facility is located on the 33rd floor of The One Tower in Barsha Heights (Tecom), offering a calm and private setting away from the city’s fast-paced lifestyle.

Registered & Professional

Effect Spa operates under a valid trade license issued by the Dubai Department of Economic Development (DED):

· Legal Entity: EFFECT GENTS SALON L.L.C

· Trade License No.: 806242

· Jurisdiction: Dubai, United Arab Emirates

We follow all applicable local regulations and maintain professional service standards, hygiene protocols, and guest care practices.

Our Services

We provide a range of non-medical wellness services focused on relaxation and comfort, including:

· Professional massage therapies

· Hammam and body care treatments

· Jacuzzi and relaxation services

All services are delivered in a respectful, professional, and appointment-based environment, with clear service boundaries.
