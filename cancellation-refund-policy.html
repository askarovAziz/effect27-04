Cancellation & Refund Policy

Appointments & Booking

At Effect Spa, all appointments are scheduled based on availability to ensure a smooth and comfortable experience for our guests. We recommend booking in advance to secure your preferred time slot.

Cancellation Policy

  • Guests are requested to inform us in advance if they wish to cancel or reschedule an appointment.
  • Cancellations made with sufficient notice will be accommodated without any issues.
  • Last-minute cancellations or no-shows may affect future booking availability.

Rescheduling Policy

  • Appointments can be rescheduled based on availability.
  • We recommend notifying our team as early as possible to arrange a suitable alternative time.

Refund Policy

  • Payments made for services are generally non-refundable once the booking is confirmed.
  • In case of any valid concern or service-related issue, our team will review the situation and provide an appropriate resolution where applicable.

Late Arrival Policy

  • Guests arriving late may receive a reduced session time to avoid delays for other scheduled appointments.
  • Full service charges may still apply in such cases.

Service Satisfaction

  • Our goal is to provide a professional and relaxing experience.
  • If you have any concerns, please inform our team during or immediately after the session so we can address them appropriately.

Right to Refuse Service

We reserve the right to refuse or discontinue service in the following cases:

  • Inappropriate behavior or misconduct
  • Violation of spa policies
  • Health or safety concerns

Policy Updates

Effect Spa reserves the right to update or modify this policy at any time without prior notice. Guests are encouraged to review this page periodically.

All services are provided as professional wellness services for relaxation purposes only. We maintain a respectful and professional environment at all times.
