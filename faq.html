FAQ – Frequently Asked Questions

  1. 1. What services does Effect Spa provide?

    Effect Spa offers a range of professional wellness services including massage therapies, hammam, jacuzzi, and relaxation treatments. All services are designed to promote comfort, relaxation, and overall well-being.

  2. 2. Are your services professional and safe?

    Yes, all services are provided by experienced therapists in a professional spa environment. We follow strict hygiene, safety, and service guidelines to ensure a comfortable and respectful experience for every guest.

  3. 3. Are your services medical treatments?

    No, all services offered at Effect Spa are non-medical wellness services intended for relaxation purposes only. They are not a substitute for medical advice or treatment.

  4. 4. How can I book an appointment?

    You can book your appointment through our website, WhatsApp, or by contacting our team directly. Our team will assist you with service selection, timing, and availability.

  5. 5. Can I choose a specific therapist?

    Therapist selection is based on availability and scheduling. If you have a preference, you may request a specific therapist during booking, and we will try to accommodate your request.

  6. 6. What information is shared during booking?

    Before confirming your appointment, we clearly communicate the treatment type, duration, pricing, and availability to ensure full transparency.

  7. 7. What is your cancellation or rescheduling policy?

    Appointments can be rescheduled or cancelled as per our cancellation policy. We recommend informing our team in advance. Please refer to our Cancellation/Refund Policy page for full details.

  8. 8. Do you offer home service?

    All services are provided at our professional spa location. For any special service requests, please contact our team directly to check availability.

  9. 9. What should I expect during my visit?

    Your session will begin with a brief consultation to understand your preferences such as pressure level and comfort. Our therapists will then provide a professional and relaxing spa experience in a calm environment.

  10. 10. How do you ensure guest privacy?

    We respect and protect the privacy of all our guests. Personal information and session details are handled with strict confidentiality in accordance with our Privacy Policy.

  11. 11. What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept standard payment methods. Details will be shared during the booking process.

  12. 12. Do you have any service guidelines?

    Yes, all services are provided within professional wellness standards. We maintain a respectful environment and reserve the right to refuse service if guidelines are not followed.

Note:

All services provided at Effect Spa are strictly professional wellness services intended for relaxation. We do not offer any inappropriate or non-professional services.
