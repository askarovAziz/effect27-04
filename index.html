About Effect Spa

Professional Spa & Wellness Center in Barsha Heights, Dubai

Welcome to Effect Spa, a professional wellness center located on the 33rd Floor, The One Tower, Barsha Heights (Tecom), Dubai. We provide professional spa treatments including hammam and relaxation services in a clean, safe, and respectful environment.

Since 2018, our business has been serving guests in Dubai with a focus on quality, consistency, and professional care. All services are delivered by experienced therapists and are tailored to individual comfort and wellness preferences.

Our goal is to provide a reliable and professional wellness experience while maintaining high standards of hygiene, privacy, and service quality.

  • Professional and operating business in Dubai, UAE
  • Professional non-medical wellness services only
  • Clean, safe, and respectful spa environment
  • Personalized service based on guest comfort
