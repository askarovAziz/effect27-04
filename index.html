The Legend of Amara

Long ago, beyond the reach of maps and memory, there stood a secret caravanserai hidden deep within the endless sands

Those who found it were not guided by roads,
but by the perfume of amber, oud, and sacred resins
drifting through the desert air.
Behind ancient stone walls and beneath cascades of heavy silk,
travelers surrendered the dust of their journeys
and the weariness of years.

Brass vessels glowed with warm light,
petals rested upon still water,
and the hush of the sanctuary wrapped around them like a timeless prayer.
There, healing was not a service, but a ritual.
Heat awakened the body, fragrance quieted the mind,
and touch restored the spirit.

Every element held meaning. Every moment invited renewal.
They named this place Amara —
a sanctuary of eternal wandering,
where beauty, stillness, and ancient wisdom meet,
and where the spirit remembers its immortal strength.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have any wellness guidance before a session?

Yes. Please consult your physician before booking if you are pregnant, recovering from surgery, or have skin sensitivities, recent discomfort, or temperature sensitivity. Our Moroccan bath uses high heat and humidity, so it may not be suitable during pregnancy or for guests sensitive to elevated temperatures. Inform our team about preferences, allergies, and recent sensitivities so we can adjust your session for maximum comfort.

When should I arrive for my session?

Please arrive 10–15 minutes early to complete any wellness notes and begin your ritual in a calm state.

What should I wear for massage and body treatments?

Comfortable clothing is recommended. Disposable items and towels are provided, and professional draping is used at all times.
