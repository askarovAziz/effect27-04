Professional service policies before your visit

Guest Information

Comfort, privacy, and a seamless wellness experience

We want every guest to enjoy a calm, secure, and premium visit. The policy below explains how we handle reservations, arrivals, cancellations, privacy, in-spa conduct, and professional wellness service boundaries so your experience is smooth from the moment you book.

Reservations

Advance booking is strongly recommended to secure your preferred time, therapist, and treatment. Reservations may be made by phone, WhatsApp, or through our contact form.

Arrival Time

Please arrive 10 to 15 minutes before your appointment so you can settle in, complete any necessary details, and begin your treatment in a relaxed state.

Cancellations

If your plans change, we kindly ask for advance notice as early as possible. Late cancellations or missed appointments may affect future booking availability during peak periods.

Health & Wellness

Please inform our team in advance about any comfort preferences, sensitivities, allergies, pregnancy, or recent procedures so we can help you choose a suitable spa service.

Privacy

Your contact details and booking information are used only to manage appointments, answer enquiries, and improve guest service. We do not share personal information unnecessarily.

Respectful Environment

Effect Spa maintains a respectful, professional, and relaxing atmosphere for every guest and therapist. We reserve the right to refuse service in case of inappropriate or disruptive behaviour.

Professional service boundaries

Effect Spa provides spa, massage, hammam, and jacuzzi services only. Requests for any inappropriate, misleading, or non-professional service are refused immediately.
