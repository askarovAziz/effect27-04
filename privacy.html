Data Transparency

Your information is used only for legitimate business communication

Effect Spa uses guest information only to respond to enquiries, arrange bookings, confirm appointments, and provide essential service updates related to our spa services.

Information we collect

Name, phone number preferred booking details, and any service notes or preferences you voluntarily share with us.

How we use information

To answer questions, confirm reservations, coordinate treatment details, and provide guest support before and after an appointment.

Communication channels

We may communicate through phone, WhatsApp when you contact us or request a booking.

We do not sell guest information. We share data only when reasonably required to operate the business, comply with legal obligations, or protect guests and staff.
